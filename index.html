Reservations

Reservations for up to 6 people can be made by telephone (62354566), e-mail info@hyygee.sg

Please Note

For parties of 5 or more, a credit card is required to reserve the table; in case of cancellation within 24 hours of the reservation, a $25 per person fee will be charged. For parties of 7 or more, please email us at info@hyygee.sg to inquire about availability. We request that parties of 7 or more participate in our tasting menu. We will do our best to accommodate all requests. We look forward to seeing you at hyyge! For press inquires, please contact Rachel at Rachel@hyygge.sg
